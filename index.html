My name is Faruk Becirovic, I was born on 15 March 2000 in Tuzla, Bosnia and Herzegovina. Since young age I have been interested in how the things work. When I was 15 I became interested in programming, I started learning C++ online and unknowingly started the process that lasts to this day. In 2018 I became involved with Tuzla based non-profit NGO called BILD, dedicated to helping and educating young people. I participated in 10th annual Tuzla Summer Institute, organised by the BILD, after which i became part of BILD-IT Training, where I learned Java, HTML, CSS, bootstrap, JavaScript, but most importantelly I became part of an incredible community of great people who want to help others and enjoy their time together. One year later, I was an assisstant teacher for the 11th Tuzla Summer Institute, from someone needing help I became someone offering my assisstance to others.

In 2019 I graduated from Behram-begova Medresa in Tuzla. Latter that year I enrolled in Faculty of Engineering and Natural Sciences at International University of Sarajevo, department of Software Engineering. Currently I am a second year student at IUS. During my studies at IUS, I became involved with BH Futres Foundation, an organisation that offers training and mentorship for young and ambitious people in Bosnia and Hercegovina. There mission is to shape the next generation of change makers by empowering the youth through education, technology and leadership. I was part of metorship program organised by BHF that lsted from December 2019 to September 2020.

November 2020 marks the begining of Codecta Academy and my first interniship opportunity. The goal of this programme is to help students become full stack development and master agile methodology for software development as well as to give them hads-on expirience from working in the field with experts from the company. Some of the technologies I had used include Quarkus, Flutter, React and Spring.
